The Minimalist

The inspiration for this template came from the hexagonal shaped graph, most commonly seen used in RPGs (role playing games). They were used to break down a character’s traits and summarize their strengths and weaknesses.
The simple layout and lack of an image keeps the template focused on it’s content.
It’s simple, concise, and effective. The Minimalist.

Samples of my previous work

Since this client had a large amount of different job experiences we replace the projects into a second experience section
Also notice that the hexagonal graph was omitted since it was not relevant or necessary.

The biggest difference here is the lack of logos. Since this client’s work was more niche, it was difficult to find a suitable image.
This is a good example of how the final product can vary between clients. With a bit of tweaking there should be no problems no matter the content.
